Luis Salas Cinematographer | Profile

I am a Cinematographer based in San José Costa Rica; I have always been interested in film and visual arts as a medium to convey ideas and a way to broaden my creative capabilities.

I studied filmmaking in Universidad VERITAS (Costa Rica) and attended The Maine Media Workshops (USA) in the fall of 2010 taking several cinematography workshops.

I work mainly in short films, documentaries, and feature film, also as an editor in my personal projects.
